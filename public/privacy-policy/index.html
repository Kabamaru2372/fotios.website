Picksy: Be Present

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026
Picksy: Be Present was built to help you, not to collect your data. This policy explains exactly what the app does — and doesn't do — with your information.

What data Picksy collects

Picksy collects the following data locally on your device only:

  • Number of phone pickups per day
  • Time spent on device per session
  • Daily and weekly pickup history
  • Your daily goal setting
  • Streak count
  • App language preference
  • Picksy Pro purchase status
This data never leaves your device. It is stored using Apple's UserDefaults and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or analytics platform.

What data Picksy does NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Health or biometric data
  • Browsing history or app usage outside of Picksy
  • Advertising identifiers

Motion & activity detection

Picksy uses CoreMotion to detect when you pick up your phone. This data is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never stored beyond the current session's pickup count. No raw motion data is retained or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

Picksy Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Picksy does not handle, store, or have access to any payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

Live Activity & Lock Screen

If enabled, Picksy displays a Live Activity on your Lock Screen showing your current pickup count. This feature uses only data already stored locally on your device and does not involve any network communication.

Third-party services

Picksy does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, or similar platforms included in the app.

Data retention & deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all Picksy data, simply delete the app from your iPhone. All associated data will be permanently removed.

Children's privacy

Picksy does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
